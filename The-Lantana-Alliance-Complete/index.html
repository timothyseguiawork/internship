Beat the heat with us this Summer!

As we approach record high temperatures this Summer, we want to make sure everyone in San Bernardino County has a safe place to rest and cool off.

All Summer long, we're setting up cooling stations all around the county. Stop by any of the locations below Monday through Sunday from 10am - 6pm to get out of the sun, drink some cold water, and enjoy some good company. We also have free hot weather supplies available: light clothes, reusable water bottles, umbrellas, and more. We'd love to see you!

No stations near you? We're working to add new stations every week, so check back!
